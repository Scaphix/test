The Regency Chronicle

Esteemed Reader,

We bid you welcome to our humble Chronicle. We present, for your diversion, a little trial of wit and memory. Within these pages you shall find select passages from the works of Miss Austen; your task, both simple and delightful, is to unite each quotation with its rightful novel. May your recollections serve you well, and your spirits remain uncommonly high.
